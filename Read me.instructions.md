# Portfolio Website

## Overview
This is a personal portfolio website designed to present professional information, services, and contact details in a clear and structured way. The site is suitable for developers, designers, or freelancers who want a simple and effective online presence.

The project is built as a static website and can be easily customized or extended.

---

## Website Structure

The portfolio contains **five main sections**, accessible through the navigation menu:

### 1. Home
The landing section introduces the portfolio and provides a quick overview of who you are and what you do.  
It typically includes:
- A short headline or tagline
- A brief introduction
- A call-to-action (e.g., View Services or Contact)

---

### 2. About
This section provides background information and professional context.

Content may include:
- Personal or professional summary
- Skills and expertise
- Experience or education highlights
- Mission or values

---

### 3. Services
The Services section outlines what you offer.

Typical elements:
- List of services or skills
- Short descriptions for each service
- Technologies or tools used
- Value provided to clients or users

---

### 4. Plan
This section presents pricing or engagement plans.

It may include:
- Service packages
- Pricing tiers
- Features included in each plan
- Conditions or scope of work

This section helps visitors understand how to work with you.

---

### 5. Contact
The Contact section allows visitors to reach out.

Common components:
- Contact form
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Social media links
- Location (optional)

---
