About Me

I’m Gauthier Cherubala, a passionate web User Interface and experience (UI & UX), Vidéo and Graphic designer, Data analyst also Marketing designer based in Bukavu Republic Democratic of Congo. Over the last 3+ years, I have worked on projects ranging from responsive websites to full product ready for delivery.

My approach combines clean visual aesthetics with a strong user-experience mindset. I love turning ideas into interactive, accessible, and visually engaging solutions.

  • Bachelor in Computer Science
  • Specialized in UI/UX & Front-end
  • Fluent in HTML, CSS, & Figma
  • Experiened in Excel, Power Bi and Power querry
  • Fluent in Marketing online tehnologies

My Mission

  • To design interfaces that not only look beautiful but also make people’s business communication flow easier. Every project is a chance to learn, experiment.
  • As a UI & UX Designer, I transform complex requirements into clear, accessible, and intuitive interfaces, always placing real users at the center of every decision. Every visual choice is guided by usability and purpose, not trends.
  • As a graphic and video designer, I create visual identities and content that tell a coherent story, strengthen the message, and capture attention without distracting from the core objective.
  • My work as a data analyst allows me to support these creative decisions with concrete data. I analyze user behavior, performance metrics, and outcomes to continuously improve products, campaigns, and user experiences.
  • Finally, as a marketing designer, I bridge creativity and strategy I design materials that are not just visually strong, but that engage, convert, and support marketing decisions based on real insights.

My goal is simple: to build cohesive digital solutions where design, data, and strategy work together to create lasting impact
